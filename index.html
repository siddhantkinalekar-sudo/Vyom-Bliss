Our Story

Born from a Grandmother's Recipe

At Vyom Bliss, our chocolate journey started in a small home kitchen in Mumbai. Dedicated to restoring the art of authentic chocolate-making, we craft premium chocolates in small batches using high-quality Belgian couverture.

We source single-origin cacao beans directly from organic farms, ensuring a pure chocolate flavor profile with zero preservatives. We pour love and meticulous luxury into every bite.
